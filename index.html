Features

Everything you need
to improve faster.

AI Pose Tracking

Live skeleton overlay

MediaPipe AI draws a skeleton on your video in real time — right in your browser, no server, no upload. See exactly where your body is at every frame.

Frame Scrubber

Frame-by-frame comparison

Scrub through both videos independently or in sync. Step one frame at a time to compare your contact point to a pro's at the exact same moment.

Slow Motion

0.1× to 2× playback speed

Slow both videos down to 10% speed to spot the small details that make a big difference — wrist snap, shoulder rotation, hip drive.

After analysis, SwingCheck gives you the top 3 things to work on based on the actual difference between your angles and your reference player's.
